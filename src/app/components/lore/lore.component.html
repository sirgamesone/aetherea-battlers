Sky Lords

A legion that guards Perifúrio and whose goal is to gain more power and use it to right the wrongs of the Aethérean species. The members of the Sky Lords feel great pride for their beliefs. It is the second of the two strongest legions in Kalítheia. The Sky Lords recently took a hit to its reputation by attempting to make peace with some of Diáthro’s factions as such actions are illegal in Kalítheia. They would have been exiled were it not for Kirin excellent leadership skills and how valuable the legion’s strength is in pursuing the geoethéreal vents. The legion is expected to make up for their criminal behavior by seizing one of Diáthro’s fortresses.

Warlords

A legion that defends Lástro and seeks absolute power over the other legions to gain militaristic power to take over Kalítheia. The Warlords of Azure is one of the two strongest legions in Kalítheia and is competing with the Sky Lords for this power. Although they are not aware of it, their lust for power extends beyond that: they will attempt to take control of Azuria, then Kalítheia, and then conquer Aethérea should they gain power or influence over the other legions. It is led by Velösek, who is favored by the government of Azuria.

Eternal Force

A legion of Diáthro’s elite soldiers who defend the seventh fortress. They seek retribution on the Kalítheians for splitting the Aethérean species and for their crimes against Aethérea.

Rhapsody

A legion whose strength lies in its raw power and ability to rush down opponents. They have an unspoken rivalry with Eternal Force as Eternal Force too possesses similar strengths.

Chaos Project

A legion of soldiers who defend the fourth fortress and are known for their voraciousness. Chaos Project has been working with the Church of Gnóstis in secret to gather Aethér form the vents and store it in a crypt for future trafficking, which violates a number of Gnóstic laws.

Ilia

A legion whose goal is to spread peace. They avoid bloodshed whenever possible, going so far as to hold defensive formations to compel the enemy to accept a stalemate. This legion possesses some of the most resilient templars. Unlike the Sky Lords, they keep their attempts at keeping the peace or neutrality under wraps. When it comes to battle, they feign incompetence at killing to create stalemates

Progma

A legion that values advancement in all forms, especially technological. They arm their soldiers with the most advanced weapons and armor that they can afford to facilitate magic.

Seeker of Lux

A cultlike legion that secretly plans to defect to Gnóstis to pursue wealth by collaborating with Aegos and the Church of Gnóstis. They will do anything that will get them closer to becoming wealthy. They pursue wealth because they are tired of living lives as soldiers and wish to lead luxurious lives.

Aegos

A legion of soldiers who defend the fourth fortress and are known for their voraciousness. Aegos has been working with the Church of Gnóstis in secret to gather Aethér form the vents and store it in a crypt for future trafficking, which violates a number of Gnóstic laws.

Heolfor

A legion of honest but lazy soldiers who are deceptively skilled in combat and are supposed to watch over the fifth fortress. They tend to spend their time having a few drinks and getting into friendly fistfights.

Godheads

A legion of soldiers who defend the third fortress. They are renowned for their good deeds, such as funding research on how to conserve Aether and facilitate the planet’s production of it. They also led a movement to aid Mag Sávres by allowing them access to Diáthro’s geoethéreal vents as a form of reparation. The fame that the Godheads amassed has corrupted their moral compass. Their actions have grown empty or detrimental and are undoubtedly motivated by their greed for fame. Furthermore, they have come to care not whether they become famous or infamous. They have since been stripped of their social status; their movement has been taken over by the Demiurge’s Warriors and is co-led by Signóma.

Ladies of Delea

A legion of female soldiers who excel at deception. They despise Aegos, for Aegos has taken a few of their women for trafficking purposes. They fear what Aegos might to do their captured members, so they keep Aegos’ crimes a secret.

Signoma

A legion of empathetic soldiers who, despite their formidability, feel an inferiority complex toward Progma. They guard the sixth fortress and are run by the Church of Gnóstis but are unaware of this.

Stalbrand

A legion of steadfast soldiers who excel at seizures. They have an unspoken rivalry with Exónas as they want to prove that they are the legion that is superior at seizing
